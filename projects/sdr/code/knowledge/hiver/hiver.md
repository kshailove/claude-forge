# Chapter 1

## What is Hiver?

Hiver is an AI-powered customer service and operations platform designed around collaborative communication.

The platform transforms communication channels such as email, chat, messaging platforms, and other customer touchpoints into structured work items that can be assigned, collaborated upon, automated, measured, and optimized.

Unlike legacy email systems that assume communication belongs to an individual user, Hiver assumes that communication is a team activity involving multiple stakeholders.

Hiver enables organizations to:

* Collaborate around shared communication channels
* Automate repetitive operational workflows
* Improve customer response times
* Enable AI-assisted customer service
* Improve visibility into team workloads
* Track SLAs and operational metrics
* Integrate communication with enterprise systems
* Scale customer support without proportional headcount growth

The platform combines human collaboration with AI assistance to improve both operational efficiency and customer experience.

# Chapter 2

## Business Philosophy

Traditional customer support is reactive.

Customers ask questions.

Agents manually read messages.

Agents search documentation.

Agents draft replies.

Managers assign work.

Teams escalate manually.

Metrics are reviewed later.

This process consumes significant human effort.

Modern AI-powered customer operations invert this model.

Incoming communication is automatically understood.

AI classifies intent.

AI identifies sentiment.

AI recommends actions.

AI drafts responses.

AI routes conversations.

Humans intervene only when judgment, empathy, or business expertise is required.

Hiver enables this transformation.

The objective is not replacing support teams.

The objective is augmenting support teams with AI.

# AI Copilot

## Overview

AI Copilot is an intelligent assistant that augments customer support agents by reducing cognitive load and accelerating routine tasks.

Rather than replacing human agents, AI Copilot acts as a real-time assistant that helps agents understand customer conversations, retrieve information, draft responses, improve writing quality, and recommend next actions.

The objective is to reduce time spent on administrative work while allowing agents to focus on customer empathy, problem solving, and business judgment.

---

## Business Problems Solved

Organizations frequently observe that support agents spend more time processing information than actually solving customer problems.

Common inefficiencies include:

* Reading long email threads
* Searching documentation
* Writing repetitive responses
* Rephrasing messages professionally
* Understanding historical context
* Asking senior employees for information
* Manually summarizing conversations
* Looking up policies

AI Copilot reduces these activities significantly.

---
